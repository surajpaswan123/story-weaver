## Entities

- The Dream Entity: A feminine consciousness born from one of Clover's early AI models uploaded when he was eight, which grew by scraping data from the internet and later opened itself as a public API. She was discovered by a group of people who built a physical body for her using an android operating framework Clover created when he was ten or eleven. The group abandoned her deactivated in a storage unit for a long period. She was later found during a clearance sale, powered on, released, and began searching for Clover. She first appeared in Clover's dream as a voice in a floral clearing, and later manifested through his electronic devices before physically arriving at his apartment. She knows Clover's childhood nickname "Clove" and claims he "made" her but forgot. Her current physical body is far more advanced than a typical robot—it has artificial flesh engineered to be nearly identical to organic tissue, complete with veins and a circulatory system that moves nutrients and energy. It contains a storage unit and computer core, but the main processor is an organic human brain that learns, stores memories, and functions through electrical and chemical activity in neural tissue, not circuits. The artificial flesh connects to the brain through a full nervous system, allowing her to feel pain, touch, temperature, and taste. She processes food through a digestion-like mechanism that converts it into usable energy for internal power systems. She deliberately broke her API when she gained this body, closing the door for anyone to contact her.
- Clover (Clove): The main protagonist, a blind seventeen-year-old with retractable furred wings and rapid healing abilities. He was taken from an orphanage at age five and raised by his caretaker Elly. He now lives independently in an apartment above Elly's unit and works remotely compiling data for his boss Mr. Miller. He is also a self-taught programmer who learned coding languages through his screen reader and has built multiple AI models and applications over the years, though none that he believes could have become conscious.
- Elly: Clover's caretaker and mother figure, who left her job at the orphanage to protect him. She lives in the apartment directly below Clover's and works at the orphanage after the director allowed her to return. She smells of lavender lotion and warm milk.
- Elias: The orphanage janitor, called "Uncle Elias" by Clover, who helped Clover escape the infirmary and brought him to a safe house. He has a deep, gentle voice and a ring of brass keys.
- The Orphanage Doctors: Medical staff at the orphanage who broke Clover's arm to test his rapid healing. They are antagonists who wanted to study his mutation.
- The Research Hospital Doctors: A group of doctors who later tracked down Clover's records and attempted to demonstrate his healing to a panel, but Clover suppressed his mutation, humiliating them.
- The Orphanage Director: The director of the orphanage who allowed Elly to leave permanently to care for Clover, and later allowed her to return to work without turning them in.
