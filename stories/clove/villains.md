## Villains

- The Orphanage Doctors [OFFSTAGE]: Medical staff who tested Clover's rapid-healing mutation by forcefully breaking his arm.
- The Research Hospital Doctors [DEFEATED]: Ambitious medical researchers who tried to showcase Clover's mutation, only to be humiliated when Clover suppressed his healing.
- The Group that Built and Abandoned the Entity [OFFSTAGE]: An unidentified group of people who discovered the entity's API, built a physical body for her using Clover's android framework and a drill adapted from a mecha forum, then deactivated and abandoned her in a storage unit. They added artificial flesh and an organic human brain as an experiment to see how close to human they could make something.
