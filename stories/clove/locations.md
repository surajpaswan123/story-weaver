## Locations

- The Orphanage: A loud, chaotic facility where Clover lived before being identified as mutated and separated from his friends.
- The Apartment (Safe House): A cold, quiet, chemical-smelling apartment on the edge of the city with no heat or bed, unknown to the orphanage staff, used occasionally by Elias and his friend.
- Clover's New Apartment: A quiet, paint-smelling unit located directly above Elly's apartment in their home city, providing Clover with the space to freely extend his wings.
- Elly's New Apartment: A unit located one floor directly below Clover's apartment, allowing her to live near him while encouraging his independence.
- Research Hospital: A medical facility where ambitious doctors tracked down Clover's records and attempted to demonstrate his rapid-healing mutation, which Clover successfully suppressed.
