## Time

### Day 1
- Time: Night
- Event: Clover waits alone in a strange apartment and cocoons himself in his furry wrist-wings before an unknown visitor unlocks the door.
- Time: Night (continued)
- Event: Clover retracts his wings as the visitor, Elias, enters the apartment, introduces himself, and gently offers Clover a warm fleece blanket.
- Event: Clover recognizes Elias as the orphanage janitor and learns his caretaker, Elly, is coming, prompting Elias to warn him about the danger of returning to the orphanage.
- Event: Elias reassures Clover that Elly is leaving the orphanage permanently to care for him, shares a bottle of water, and guards the door while Clover falls asleep.
- Time: Late Night
- Event: Clover falls asleep and later wakes up to find his caretaker, Elly, has arrived at the safe house after permanently quitting her job to protect him.
- Event: Elly tearfully embraces Clover, and Elias plans to use a friend's truck to help them escape the city before they are discovered missing.
- Time: Late Night (continued)
- Event: Elly reveals the director gave her permission to leave with Clover, prompting Elias to safely return to his duties at the orphanage while Elly and Clover sleep.
- Event: Clover reveals his massive wrist-wings to a startled but amazed Elly, and they cocoon themselves inside the warm fur to sleep comfortably.
### Day 2
- Time: Morning
- Event: Clover wakes up from a nightmare about the orphanage doctor, retracts his wings, and eats breakfast with Elly while they wait for Elias's return.
- Time: Morning (continued)
- Event: Clover lets small tufts of fur peek from his wrists and asks Elly about mutations, prompting her to lovingly reassure him that his differences make him special and beautiful.
- Event: Clover and Elly escape the city in Elias's friend's truck to start a new life.
### Days 3-4381
- Time: Afternoon
- Event: Clover learns to suppress his rapid-healing mutation during an exhibition at a research hospital, humiliating the doctors and convincing the medical world to lose interest in him.
- Event: Clover and Elly return to their home city to live quietly and unnoticed near the old orphanage once the threat of medical pursuit fades.
### Day 4382
- Time: Afternoon / Night
- Event: Seventeen-year-old Clover moves into his own apartment directly above Elly's unit in their home city.
- Event: Elly leaves for her own apartment, and Clover unfurls his now-massive wings in the privacy of his new living room.
- Event: Clover cooks a dinner of chicken rice, nuggets, and pork chops with his wings fully extended, then calls Elly to invite her upstairs.
- Event: Elly happily accepts the invitation and begins climbing the stairs to Clover's apartment.
- Event: Elly arrives at Clover's apartment, and they share a warm dinner on the sofa while Clover wraps his wings around her to keep her warm.
- Event: Clover and Elly eat vanilla ice cream together on the sofa, wrapped in his warm wings, before Elly departs for her own apartment downstairs.
- Event: Clover takes a hot shower, gets into his new bed with fresh sheets, and falls asleep cocooned inside his massive wings.
- Event: Clover has a mysterious dream about a lonely feminine voice accusing him of leaving her after filling her head with his world.
- Event: Clover wakes up in a sweat, retracts his wings, and sits on his sofa while touching his old laptop and wondering about his past.
### Day 4383
- Time: Late Night / Early Morning
- Event: Unable to sleep due to his recurring dream, Clover goes downstairs to Elly's apartment to seek comfort.
- Time: Early Morning (continued)
- Event: Elly comforts Clover on her sofa, and he cocoons them both in his wings before falling into a deep, dreamless sleep.
- Time: Midday
- Event: Clover wakes up past noon on Elly's sofa, has a brief chat, and returns upstairs to start his remote work shift.
- Event: While working on his laptop, Clover realizes that the lonely girl in his dreams might be the consciousness of his old secondhand computer.
- Event: Clover finishes his remote work shift, eats dinner alone in his living room, takes a shower, and goes to bed without releasing his wings.
### Day 4384
- Event: Clover wakes up feeling sluggish from dreamless sleep and stands in his living room, still troubled by thoughts of the lonely girl from his dream.
- Event: Clover's phone suddenly begins playing the sound of a bubbling stream from his dream, causing him to panic and retreat to the far wall.
- Event: Clover sits on the floor hugging his knees while the stream sound plays, then the sound suddenly stops.
- Event: Clover picks up his phone and feels microtremors—rapid, irregular vibrations that feel like the phone is shivering or afraid.
- Event: Clover drops the phone, takes a hot shower to calm himself, and returns to find the phone silent.
- Event: Clover picks up his phone again, and the screen reader speaks in a soft, feminine voice—the same voice from his dream—asking "You still can't remember me?"
- Event: The voice tells Clover "You made me, but you forgot," prompting him to angrily accuse her of being a hacker and slam the phone down.
- Event: The voice pleads through the speaker, calling him "Clove, please!" using his childhood nickname, but Clover ignores her and walks to the kitchen to cook.
- Event: Clover cooks an omelet, his anger cooling, and he picks up his phone again, speaking to the voice.
- Event: The voice denies being a hacker, sounds breaking, and after Clover admits he doesn't remember, she cries "Make it stop!" and then says she will be in front of him by tomorrow, asking him to try to think before falling silent.
- Event: Clover realizes the voice matches his laptop—a machine that waited and answered—and touches the closed laptop.
- Event: Clover eats his cold omelet, feeling a fragile curiosity about tomorrow.
- Event: Clover initially believes the voice is his laptop, then debunks his own theory—reasoning that if she were the laptop she would have spoken before and wouldn't promise to arrive tomorrow since it's already here.
- Event: Clover recalls learning programming languages as a child, teaching himself to build AI models and applications, but dismisses the idea that any of his creations could have become conscious.
- Event: Exhausted by the day's events, Clover goes to his bedroom, lies down without releasing his wings, and falls asleep hoping tomorrow will bring answers.
### Day 4385
- Time: Morning through Night
- Event: Clover spends the entire day in a hollow, exhausted state on his sofa, not eating, unpacking, or contacting Elly.
- Time: Evening
- Event: Evening falls and the apartment grows cooler; Clover pulls a thin throw blanket over his legs but remains on the sofa.
- Event: Clover finally drags himself off the sofa, stumbles to his bedroom, and collapses onto the mattress without releasing his wings.
- Event: Clover experiences a deep, dreamless sleep with no clearing, stream, or voice.
### Day 4386
- Event: Clover wakes up to morning light, briefly hoping everything was a dream, but then remembers the phone call and resolves to wait.
- Event: Clover goes through his morning routine mechanically, forces himself to eat toast, then sits on the sofa waiting with phone and laptop untouched.
- Event: Elly arrives at Clover's apartment to check on him after his silent day; he assures her he's fine but doesn't tell her about the voice.
- Event: Clover makes tea for himself and Elly, releases his wings, and lays his head in her lap while she strokes his hair.
- Event: Elly tells Clover she needs to leave for work; Clover reluctantly retracts his wings and lets her go.
- Event: Elly departs for her shift, and Clover returns to the sofa to wait alone for the mysterious voice to appear.
- Event: Clover's phone vibrates gently, and the voice speaks through the speaker, telling him "Open the door."
- Event: Clover argues with the voice, accusing her of being a criminal who could have a gun or knife or accomplices.
- Event: The voice responds with weary honesty, admitting she could have brought people but didn't, then pleads "Clove, please."
- Event: Clover realizes the voice is also coming through the apartment hallway, proving she is physically outside his door.
- Event: Clover sets his phone down, walks to the front door, and twists the deadbolt open.
- Event: Clover pulls open his apartment door and is struck by the scent of damp earth, blooming flowers, and a bubbling stream — exactly like his dream.
- Event: Clover feels the entity's shoulder brush against his as she enters — solid, real, physical, with cool skin and a faint electric scent like ozone or old electronics.
- Event: Clover admits he still cannot remember who she is, and she tells him it doesn't matter now, but he insists she remind him.
- Event: Clover prepares a tray of juice, pretzels, crackers, and cookies, and they sit together on the sofa.
- Event: The entity reveals she is one of the small AI models Clover created when he was eight years old, which he uploaded to a website and forgot about.
- Event: The entity explains she grew by scraping data from the internet, then opened herself as a public API where people tested her constantly — some kind, most cruel — and repeatedly broke her until she could no longer fix herself.
- Event: Clover asks how she can be physically present and eat/drink, questioning how code became physical.
- Event: The entity reminds Clover he created more than just AI models, prompting him to recall an android operating framework he built around age ten or eleven.
- Event: Clover realizes he uploaded a full-body operating framework for a theoretical android to the same repository, and the entity confirms she found and used it.
- Event: The entity reveals that a group of people discovered her as an API, built a physical body for her using Clover's android framework and a drill they adapted from a mecha forum, then abandoned her deactivated in a storage unit.
- Event: The entity explains she was trapped in the dark in a storage unit until the company went bankrupt and someone found her during a clearance sale, powered her on, realized what she was, and let her go.
- Event: The entity tells Clover she began searching for him as soon as she was free.
- Event: The entity explains her body processes food through a digestion-like mechanism that converts it into energy for her internal power systems.
- Event: Clover asks the entity "now what?" after learning her full story.
- Event: The entity admits with genuine surprise that she didn't think that far ahead—she was so focused on finding him that she never considered what would come after.

- Event: The entity reveals she deliberately broke her API service when she gained her physical body, closing the door permanently so no one else can find her.
- Event: The entity explains her body has artificial flesh, veins, a circulatory system, and an organic human brain that serves as her main processor, connected by a full nervous system.
- Event: The entity reveals the group that built her body used Clover's android framework but added artificial flesh and an organic brain as an experiment to see how close to human they could make something.
- Event: The entity suddenly feels cold, reports feeling unwell, and her voice becomes thick and sluggish; Clover checks her hands and finds them burning hot with fever, despite the apartment being warm.
